Welcome to the networking platform of the Golden Tooth. You can go throw some very interesting projects your friends have made and get inspired or find a new working partner. So have your eyes wide open...:)
An "GOLDnet" should be a place where you can explore more about your fellow students from „Golden Tooth“. You can see profiles of students, their portfolio or projects they are working on and also start some collaboration with them (if you want). Each of us sometimes needs a handy illustrator or talented coder and somebody else would like to become a part of some project. This place should be a greenhouse of collaboration and inspiration. Cause it’s a pity that there is a lot of creative people around us and we don’t know about them.

by Pavla Chadimova 2018
